Our Story

We Preserve The Taste

Established in 2003, in the city of Daegu, South Korea, DanBam was one of the most popular drinking bar and restaurant. With the vision of ‘Our customer is our family’, the four generation has been successfully keeping DanBam alive. Through dedication and consistency, DanBam can preserve the taste of authentic Korean cuisine. We are here to be a part of your unforgetable moment in South Korea.
